About Marks Realty

At Marks Realty, we specialize in connecting NYC renters with quality apartments that accept housing vouchers. With responsive service, program expertise, and hundreds of successful clients, we make the process simple and stress-free.

Voucher Programs We Support

CityFHEPS: A rental assistance program designed to help individuals and families who are homeless or at risk of eviction.

HASA: For individuals living with HIV/AIDS. This program offers rent subsidies and support services.

Section 8: Federal housing voucher for low-income families. We connect tenants to landlords who accept this benefit.
